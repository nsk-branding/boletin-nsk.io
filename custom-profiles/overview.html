Custom Profiles for Utherverse

Overview

Welcome to NSK Creative Solutions! We specialize in creating unique and personalized profiles that reflect your individual preferences and style. This document outlines the process, design preferences, payment details, and additional options available for your custom profile.

1. Design Preferences

When creating your custom profile, we want to ensure it truly represents you. Here are some questions to consider:

  • Color Preferences: What specific colors do you want to incorporate into your profile?
  • Theme Selection: Do you have a theme in mind? For example:
    • Motorcycles
    • Volcanoes
    • Mountains
    • Boats
    • Or any other interests you may have
  • Profile Style: Would you prefer a more commercial profile or a personal touch? You can also choose a mix of both!
  • Seasonal Backgrounds: We can update your profile background to reflect different holidays or seasons (e.g., Halloween, Christmas) for a fresh look.

2. Music and Multimedia

While it’s generally not recommended to add background music automatically (as many users prefer profiles without it), we can include it if it holds special significance to you, such as a karaoke recording or a sentimental song.

3. Payment and Delivery

We operate on an advance payment system to streamline the process:

  • Payment Structure:
    • 50% upfront (30 rays) is required before we start.
    • The remaining 50% (30 rays) is due upon delivery of the final code.
  • Delivery Time: The standard delivery time is one week from the date of receiving the deposit.

4. Maintenance and Updates

  • Monthly Maintenance: You are entitled to one maintenance or update of your profile each month at no additional cost.
  • Additional Updates: Any updates beyond the monthly maintenance will incur a fee of 10 rays.
  • Newsletter Maintenance: For newsletters, maintenance is also free, and you can request updates as many times as you need.

5. Sharing Images and Certificates

If you’d like to include photographs, certificates, or any other images in your profile, please send the links or files via Skype. This makes the process smoother and allows for better communication throughout the project.

Conclusion
